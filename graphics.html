Mascot

Character

The Slotion mascot is an illustration of a sloth, an animal that symbolizes gentleness and relaxation, representing Slotion’s objective to provide a “slow-fashion solution”. Textured strokes that outline the mascot add a theme of playfulness and creativity.

Colour

Similar to the logo, Slotion’s mascot also uses tints of green from Slotion’s colour palette.
