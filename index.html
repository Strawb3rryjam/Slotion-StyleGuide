What is Slotion?

Slotion is a social media platform for outfit discovery, creation, and sharing. Our objective is to promote sustainability with slow-fashion solutions, by providing our users with alternatives to buying a new piece of clothing or throwing it away.

Closet in your hands

Slotion’s closet saves you hours by providing quick access to your real-life wardrobe, where you can add and combine items to create an outfit without having to try them on.

Fitcheck Made Easier

Share your outfit along with a photo for a fitcheck. Your friends can see details of each piece without having to bother you for info!
