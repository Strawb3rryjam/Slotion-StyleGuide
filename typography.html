Typography

Why Poppins?

We decided to use Poppins as our primary typeface because it is a simple and clean sans serif typeface. This minimalistic style goes well with our branding. We wanted to keep our designs as simple as possible because we didn’t want to take away from one of the apps main focus’, style and fashion.

Why Outfit?

Outfit is our secondary typeface, mainly used for our logo, but can be found in a few headers throughout the app.
